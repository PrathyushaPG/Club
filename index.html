Rotract Club

Rotaract (Rotary-in-action) club of Presidency College (Autonomous) (Re accredited by NAAC with A+ Grade) extends selfless services towards the society. Many like-minded people get together for the same cause and develop in every aspect possible be it individually, professionally or emotionally. Rotaract brings together people aged between 18-30 to exchange ideas, develop leadership and professional skills, and have fun through service

RaCPC believes in working towards perfection with love, compassion and empathy towards the society. Every member of the club is inevitably a crucial part of the Rotaract family worldwide. RaCPC is not just confined to Presidency College (Autonomous) but also out of the campus by conducting events from District to International level. Many different individuals come together for their mutual concern towards the society and begin to work towards a better society with unity, peace and harmony.

A harmonious society is often created by responsible citizens and leaders, Troopers offers a platform to learn, grow, and evolve as young leaders who envision to create a better tomorrow.
